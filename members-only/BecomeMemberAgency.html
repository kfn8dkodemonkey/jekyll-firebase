Becoming a Member Agency will involve at a minimum, reviewing and signing:

  • The CISC Intergovernmental Agreement (IGA)
  • A Joinder to the CISC/Numerica Regional Data Warehouse (RDW) Agreement
  • A Memorandum of Understanding and addendum for NCIS/LInX
  • All detailed information provided following submission of the application Form
